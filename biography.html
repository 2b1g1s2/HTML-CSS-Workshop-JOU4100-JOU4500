Who is This Guy?

Hello, my name is Brian Geagea-Sharbin, I am an amateur photographer, videographer, and graphic designer from the Gatineau-Ottawa region.

I fell in love with photography while attending Algonquin College's journalism program, where learning how to use a DSLR camera was required. From there, I caught the photo bug and decided to pursue it further.

While you can see some samples of my work on my "Media" page, take a look at my Twitter and Instagram profiles for more examples.
